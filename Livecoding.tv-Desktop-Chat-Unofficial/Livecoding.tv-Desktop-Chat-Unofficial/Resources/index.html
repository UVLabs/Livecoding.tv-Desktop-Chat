LiveCoding.tv Desktop Chat App.

Coded by TheLoneDeveloper.

This is a simple wrapper for the livecoding.tv website's popout chat.

How To Use:

  • - Click the button below
  • - Log into your livecoding.tv account
  • - Go to your channel.
  • - Click popout chat button.
  • - Chat freely from one monitor with your audience!.
